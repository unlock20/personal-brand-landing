THE 30-DAY ROADMAP

One call a week. Four weeks to a brand.

WEEK 1 · DAY 1–7

Foundation — Why, What & How

We start with your WHY — the real reason you want to create content, who you want to reach, and how you want to help them. From there you'll choose your WHAT: a focused niche, or your own "sauce recipe" — the specific mix of traits, stories, and interests that narrows the pool of people who fit it down to exactly one person: you. We close the week by locking in your HOW — the content pillars (educate, inspire, relate, entertain) that fit your what. By day 7, you know why you're making content, what it's about, and how you'll create it.

WEEK 2 · DAY 8–14

Voice — Messaging, Storytelling & Documenting

With your foundation set, we build the voice behind it. This week covers messaging and copy that sound like you, storytelling and documenting as ways to bring your pillars to life, and the habits that separate creators who stay consistent from ones who burn out — including banking content and building a catalogue that grows daily, so you're never starting from zero on shoot day.

WEEK 3 · DAY 15–21

Ideation — 100 Video Concepts

You bring 50 short-form video concepts. Jordan brings 50 more. Together that's a guaranteed 100 ideas to work with. We compare, cut, and combine until 15–20 are selected as the strongest, most ready-to-shoot concepts to carry you into launch week.

WEEK 4 · DAY 22–30

Execution — Post Daily for 7 Days

Everything comes together. Using the concepts you selected, you post daily for the final 7 days of the program — turning a month of clarity into a live, running content practice you carry forward on your own.
